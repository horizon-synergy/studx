✦ Features

Everything you need to trade on campus

StudX packs everything student sellers and buyers need into one clean, fast, mobile-first experience.

📦

Create Listings

Photograph your item, add a price and tags, and go live in under a minute. Reach every student on your campus instantly.

🔍

Browse Products

Filter by category, search by tag, and discover featured listings. Find exactly what you need without the noise.

🔐

Google Sign-In

Log in with your student Google account in one tap. No new passwords to remember, no long sign-up forms.

⚡

Real-Time Marketplace

Listings, messages and order updates arrive instantly via Firestore. The marketplace is always live and always current.

✨

Modern Experience

A dark gallery aesthetic, smooth animations and a clean layout make browsing feel premium, not cluttered.

🎓

Student-Focused

Coupons, verified sellers, wishlist, reviews and a full order lifecycle built around how students actually trade.

🎓
Built for Student Life
From selling last year's textbooks to offering tutoring sessions, StudX fits every part of the student economy.

✦ Why StudX

Your campus economy, finally organised

Generic marketplaces were not built with students in mind. StudX is. Every feature exists to make buying, selling and connecting within your community easier, safer and faster.

🔒
Safe and Trustworthy
Verified seller badges, mutual trade confirmation and admin payment oversight keep every transaction protected.
💬
Direct Communication
Message sellers in real time before you commit. Ask questions, negotiate prices and agree on handover details.
📊
Transparent Orders
Track every step from placement to delivery. Both buyer and seller confirm the trade before it closes.
🆓
Free to Use
No listing fees, no commission cuts. StudX is free for every student on every campus.

✦ FAQ

Common questions

Cannot find an answer? Reach us at support@studx.app

StudX has verified seller badges, mutual trade confirmation and admin payment oversight. Every transaction requires both parties to confirm completion, so neither side is left in the dark. We also monitor disputes and review flagged listings.
Download the app and tap Sign In with Google to use your existing student Google account, or register with any email address. The whole process takes under 30 seconds.
Tap the + button in the navigation bar. Add up to 5 photos, write a title, set a price, choose a category, and add tags so buyers can find you. Your listing goes live the moment you hit Publish.
Buyers and sellers agree on payment terms directly. Once the buyer confirms they have received the item, the admin releases the payment record. StudX is currently a coordination and trust layer, not a payment processor. We recommend EFT, cash on handover or platforms like SnapScan.
Anything a student would legitimately buy or sell: textbooks, notes, electronics, clothing, tutoring, design services, coding help, and more. Listings for illegal goods, counterfeit items or anything that violates our terms are removed immediately.
Either party can raise a dispute from the order detail screen. Once a dispute is raised, our admin team reviews the case and steps in to resolve it. We take every dispute seriously and aim to respond within 24 hours.
Yes. StudX is completely free to download, browse and list on. There are no listing fees and no commission on sales. We believe students deserve a marketplace that does not take a cut of their hustle.
Send us an email at support@studx.app. If you are a student with a billing issue or a safety concern, include your order ID and we will prioritise your case. We also respond via the in-app chat feature.
